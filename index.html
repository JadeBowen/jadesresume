About Me

My journey into the world of telecommunications began early in life. At the age of 12, I had the opportunity to visit a cell phone site with my father, a seasoned telecommunications engineer. Witnessing the intricate workings of wireless communication sparked a fascination that has guided my career ever since. From that moment, I was captivated by the power of technology to connect people and bridge distances.

Growing up, my father instilled in me not only a passion for telecommunications but also the values of patience, precision, and dedication. His words, "Take the time to get it right, because what we build connects people," have become a guiding principle throughout my career.

With over 21 years of experience in the construction and telecommunications industries, I've had the privilege of contributing to projects that have a real-world impact. From overseeing large-scale installations to leading diverse teams, I've consistently strived to build systems that enhance connectivity and improve lives. As a single father of four, I've also mastered the art of multitasking and time management, skills that have proven invaluable in both my personal and professional life. My ultimate passion lies in leveraging my expertise to create meaningful connections and contribute to a more connected world.
